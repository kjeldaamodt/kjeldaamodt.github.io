For more than 50 years, the UCSF Orthodontic Clinic has provided excellent care for generations of patients and their families in San Francisco.


The patient experience at UCSF is somewhat different from the typical private practice. At each office visit, our patients are attended by both a resident and a member of the faculty.


At UCSF, teaching and education are important parts of our mission. We focus on the best possible treatment for our patients using the most modern orthodontic technology.
